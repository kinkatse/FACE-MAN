How To Use! Enable your camera, now try opening your mouth, closing your eyes or trying a filter! You can try a filter by clicking any filter button and then if you would like to switch, either click the same one to toggle it off or clear. Actually, some can be combined depending on if they fit so test some of them together and see what happens!
